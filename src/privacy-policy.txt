Privacy Policy for DoThis Chrome Extension

Effective Date: 16/01/2024

1. Overview

Welcome to DoThis, a Chrome extension designed to help you organize tasks and improve your work-life balance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using DoThis, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection

User-Generated Content: DoThis allows you to create and manage to-do lists, tasks, and related content. Your data is stored locally within the extension to provide you with the task management functionality.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the extension, such as when you create, edit, or delete tasks. This data helps us improve the user experience.

3. Data Usage

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

To provide you with task management features and functionality.
To enhance and personalize your experience with DoThis.
To improve our extension's performance and features based on user feedback.
4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally within the extension and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties.
We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
5. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your user-generated content within the extension. To do so, use the features provided by DoThis.

6. Third-Party Services

DoThis does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs, and we do not share your data with third parties.

7. Cookies and Tracking

DoThis does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any changes will be posted within the extension.
