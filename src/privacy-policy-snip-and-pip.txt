Privacy Policy for Snip & PiP Chrome Extension
Effective Date: 16/01/2024

1. Overview
Welcome to Snip & PiP, a Chrome extension that allows you to snip any part of your tab or pop out entire content into a floating window for seamless multitasking. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using Snip & PiP, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

2. Data Collection
User-Generated Content: Snip & PiP enables users to select and display parts of their browser tabs in a floating window. This selection is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted externally.
Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data regarding feature usage (e.g., how often PiP mode is activated) to improve functionality. However, this data does not contain personally identifiable information.
3. Data Usage
We use the collected data to:

Provide snipping and floating window functionality for enhanced multitasking.
Optimize and improve user experience based on feedback.
Maintain extension performance and ensure smooth operation.
4. Data Storage and Security
Your selections and snips are processed locally and are never stored on our servers.
We do not transmit, share, or sell your data to any third party.
We implement security measures to protect the integrity of your browsing experience.
5. User Rights
You have the right to:

Control, modify, or stop using the extension at any time.
Remove all extension-related data by uninstalling Snip & PiP.
6. Third-Party Services
Snip & PiP does not integrate with third-party services or APIs, and we do not share your data with any external entities.

7. Cookies and Tracking
Snip & PiP does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics services that collect personal data.

8. Auto-Heal Feature
Our extension provides an Auto-Heal button that resets the extension if it encounters any issues. This ensures a smooth experience without requiring manual troubleshooting.

9. Updates to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted within the extension settings.
