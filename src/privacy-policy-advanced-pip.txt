1. Overview
Welcome to Advanced PiP, a Chrome extension designed to enhance your video-watching experience by enabling Picture-in-Picture mode with playback controls. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and protect your privacy while using Advanced PiP.

2. Data Collection
Advanced PiP does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

Specifically, we do not collect:
Personally Identifiable Information (such as name, email address, or identification numbers)
Health information
Financial and payment information
Authentication information
Personal communications
Location information
Web history
User activity (such as clicks, mouse position, or keystroke logging)
Website content (text, images, videos, or hyperlinks)
3. Data Usage
Since Advanced PiP does not collect any user data, there is no data usage.

4. Data Storage and Security
All operations of Advanced PiP occur locally within your browser.
No data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.
5. User Rights
As Advanced PiP does not store any data, there is no need for data access, modification, or deletion rights.

6. Third-Party Services
Advanced PiP does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

7. Cookies and Tracking
Advanced PiP does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted within the extension.
